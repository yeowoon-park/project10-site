Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Project10 is a mobile game developed for research and experimental purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected and used when you use the app.

Data We Collect

The app collects anonymous usage data, including gameplay interactions, scores, session duration, and aggregated advertising analytics. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising Data

The app may collect aggregated information about ad impressions and interactions for analytics and research purposes. This data does not include advertising identifiers, is not used for cross-app tracking, and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Diagnostics

The app may collect crash logs and performance data to improve stability and app functionality.

How We Use Data

  • Analytics and research purposes
  • Improving app functionality and stability

Data Sharing

Data may be processed by third-party service providers such as advertising and analytics SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) solely for the purposes described above.

Your Privacy

The app does not collect names, email addresses, precise location data, or other personal identifiers. The data collected cannot be used to identify individual users.
